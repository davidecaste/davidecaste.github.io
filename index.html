Davide Castelnovo

I am a PhD student in the Department of Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science at the University of Udine. My supervisor is Marino Miculan.

My main research area is category theory and its interaction with algebra and with theoretical computer science.

In June I'll start working in the Department of Mathematics of the University of Padova.

Ongoing projects

  • Together with Luca Geatti I'm writing a paper on Krohn-Rhodes Theorem.
  • I'm trying to use Mitchell Theorem. to characterize epimorphisms in various categories.
  • Closure operators can be used to provide a semantics to SLCS.
  • It would be nice to have a general framework in which to accomodate various notions of entropy.
  • I'm working with Greta Coraglia, Fosco Loregian and Ülo Reimaa on a thing called the fibration of algebras.
